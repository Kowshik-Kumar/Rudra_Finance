## 📧 How It Works

1. Customer fills contact form on your website
2. Form is submitted
3. **You receive email immediately** with:
   - Customer's name
   - Email address  
   - Phone number
   - Service they want
   - Their complete message
4. You reply or call them back

---

## 📱 Get Notifications on Your Phone

### iPhone Users:
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Tap **Mail**
3. Tap **Notifications**
4. Turn ON "Allow Notifications"
5. Done! You'll get instant alerts 📱

### Android Users:
1. Open **Gmail app**
2. Tap menu (three lines)
3. Tap **Settings**
4. Select your email account
5. Turn ON "Notifications"
6. Done! You'll get instant alerts 📱
